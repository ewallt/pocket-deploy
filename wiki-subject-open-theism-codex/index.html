Open Theism

A relational model of divine providence that highlights dynamic omniscience, genuine human freedom, and God’s responsive guidance.

These consolidated study notes synthesize the Colorful Reader Open Theism Codex into three long-form explorations. Each page gathers five former installments into a single scrollable article with anchored sections for quick reference.

  • Introduction and Core Tenets Defines the movement’s theological commitments, reexamines time and foreknowledge, and profiles responsive providence.
  • Scriptural Basis, History, and Key Debates Surveys prophetic interpretation, Old and New Testament narratives, historical precedents, and contrasting doctrines.
  • Criticisms, Implications, and Contemporary Relevance Collects major objections, compares alternative models, and assesses pastoral, philosophical, and present-day significance.
