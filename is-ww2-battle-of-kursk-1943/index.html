Battle of Kursk (1943)

Core Summary

From 5 July to 23 August 1943, German forces launched Operation Citadel against the Kursk salient to regain the initiative after Stalingrad. Deep Soviet defenses, massive minefields, and layered reserves blunted the offensive at Orel (north) and Belgorod/Prokhorovka (south). Follow-on Soviet offensives smashed the overextended Wehrmacht, securing strategic initiative on the Eastern Front for the remainder of the war.
