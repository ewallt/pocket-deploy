Behold Your God — Series I: God’s Character and the Great Controversy

Scripture and Ellen White as used in Behold Your God — unveiling the truth about God’s character, how it was misrepresented, and how love triumphs over force.

4 — The Law of Love as the Law of Life

The law as the principle of life and freedom; sin as separation from the Source.

5 — The Great Controversy: Truth Versus Force

Two governments revealed; love persuades, force coerces.
