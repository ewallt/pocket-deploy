Part 2 — God on Trial

The accusations: God is arbitrary, self-seeking, unsafe. The response cannot be force—it must be transparent truth.

The Charge Sheet: If God seeks His own advantage, creatures are tools; fear replaces trust.

Why Not Overrule? A forced verdict convinces no one. Only patient demonstration can answer moral claims.

Revelation as Evidence: Across history God acts openly, inviting examination rather than demanding submission.

Toward the Cross: The case will turn not on argument but on a life laid down for enemies.
