Grace That Liberates: A Jesus-Centered Gospel

How the Good News Reframes God, Sin, Faith, and the Church

Core Thesis

The gospel is not a sin-management plan or a religious ladder; it is the announcement that God is exactly like Jesus and that His relentless grace is already reaching for every person (John 14:9; Luke 19:10). In Jesus we see that God condemns sin because it destroys us, yet He refuses to condemn sinners‚ÄîHe forgives, restores, and invites us to follow Him into real life (John 8:11; Luke 15). The church exists not to guard boundaries but to embody hospitality, reconciliation, and practical mercy that mirror the Father‚Äôs heart (2 Corinthians 5:18‚Äì20; Matthew 9:13).
