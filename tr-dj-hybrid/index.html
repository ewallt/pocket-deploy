      Hybrid & In-Person Roles Are Widely Available

      Salt Lake City offers abundant hybrid and on-site options across finance and accounting, with remote still present but less common.

      • Role mix: ~47% hybrid, ~40% fully in-person, ~13% fully remote.
      • Remote-friendly market: ~18.5% of the SLC workforce works remotely vs ~14% nationally—finance and insurance are major contributors.
      • Growth backdrop: Utah nonfarm payroll up ~1.8–1.9% (2024–2025), adding tens of thousands of jobs.

      Current Hybrid Roles Targeted to Your Background

      • Fund Finance Assistant Vice President (Hybrid): ~$90K–$120K; accounting and SEC compliance for registered funds.
      • Senior Manager, Risk Operations (Hybrid): ~$112K–$144K; risk controls in a payments/fintech environment.
      More senior hybrid options
      • Finance & Accounting – Custom App Dev, Senior Manager (Remote/Hybrid): ~$124K–$280K; lead data modeling for finance/actuarial use cases.
      • CFO-level (Outsourced Accounting, Construction) (Remote/Hybrid): ~$125K–$185K; advisory scope plus team mentoring.
      • Senior Director, Card & Banking Market Strategy (Hybrid): ~$167K–$250K; strategy and forecasting in fintech/big-data.

      How to Search Strategically for These Roles

      • Job boards: ZipRecruiter, BuiltIn, Glassdoor—filter for hybrid finance, finance hybrid, hybrid accounting.
      • Active SLC sectors: fintech (e.g., SoFi, TransUnion), traditional finance (Zions Bancorp), healthcare, and higher education.
      • Trend watch: Goldman Sachs’ continued back-/middle-office presence (e.g., initiatives like “Project Voyage”) can surface senior hybrid paths.
      Search filters & saved queries
      • "finance" AND "hybrid" AND (analyst OR manager OR controller) AND "Salt Lake City"
      • "accounting" AND (hybrid OR flexible) AND (GAAP OR SOX OR "process improvement")
      • "FP&A" AND "hybrid" AND (forecast OR variance OR "3-statement")

      How to Present Your Hybrid Preference Effectively

      • Frame hybrid as a balance: on-site for leadership, cross-functional alignment, and mentoring; remote for deep analytical work.
      • Show outcomes from past hybrid models (close days ↓, forecast accuracy ↑, audit findings ↓, adoption of automation ↑).
      • Normalize flexibility but signal enthusiasm for in-person strategy sessions and team rhythms.
      Sample phrasing you can reuse
      • Resume: “Led hybrid finance team across two sites; shortened monthly close by 3 days while increasing forecast accuracy from 82%→93%.”
      • Cover letter: “I prefer a hybrid cadence—on-site for leadership and collaboration, remote for focused modeling—that’s proven to raise quality and speed.”
      • Interview: “My hybrid rhythm reserves in-person time for decision cycles and mentoring; remote blocks are for variance diagnostics and model iteration.”

      Why This Works for You

      • Your finance operations leadership, process improvement, forecasting, and audit background map directly to SLC’s hybrid-ready senior roles.
      • Momentum in SLC’s finance/fintech ecosystem increases the surface area for hybrid leadership positions.
      • Demonstrating flexibility and willingness to be present differentiates you among senior candidates.
